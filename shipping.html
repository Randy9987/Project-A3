Shipping

Free shipping on orders over $300 AUD!*

Is your order time sensitive? Let us know by leaving a note in your cart!

All fabric, supplies and sample orders placed through our Online Store, are processed and dispatched from our New Zealand warehouse which is separate to all of our physical store locations.

If you are near one of our stores in Australia, it may be quicker for you to place a mail order directly with the store nearest you. You’ll just need to check whether the store has the stock available that you are wanting to purchase, and what the cost of local postage will be. Pricing for mail orders from our stores varies depending on the size of your order.

We aim to cut and dispatch orders within 10 working days. If you have placed an order during a promotional period, this dispatch timeframe may swell up to 14 days. Please refer to your order confirmation email during sale time for a more accurate estimate. Please note, once dispatched, the courier is responsible for delivery and all deliveries are contactless unless otherwise requested.

Australia Online Shipping

Aus Post (Parcel Post) Standard:

  • Flat Rate: $15
  • Metro: 5–9 working days from dispatch
  • Rural: 5–12 working days from dispatch
  • Service: Tracked

If not at home, drivers may leave your parcel in a secure spot. Otherwise, a card will be left. If a signature is required, leave a note in your cart or email us after purchase. PO Boxes and Parcel Lockers are supported.

NZ Post manages this service. Tracking will be via NZ Post. Parcels originate from a NZ Post depot in NSW but are shipped from our Auckland HQ.

  • Rural: 3–5 working days from dispatch
  • Service: Tracked

Authority to leave applies unless otherwise stated. No PO Box delivery — use a physical address.

DHL shipments originate from NZ. $25 flat rate is subsidised. For in-store options, contact your nearest TFS location. See store details

Samples – Free Untracked NZ Post:

  • Price: Free
  • Service: Untracked

Delivery in 10–30 working days. Delays may occur. If purchased with fabric, samples ship together via tracked courier.

Duties & Customs

Australia import threshold: $1000 AUD. Orders above this may incur duties/taxes payable by the customer. Contact us before placing large orders if unsure.

Duties are based on goods + shipping cost, even if shipping is free. Customs policies vary by country.

Courier Limitations

  • DHL does not deliver to PO boxes or parcel lockers
  • Australia Post does support PO boxes and parcel lockers
